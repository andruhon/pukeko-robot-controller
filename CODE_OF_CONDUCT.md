# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, welcoming, and considerate.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully, and offer it kindly.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, and
discussions — and whenever an individual is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainers
through the channel in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). Maintainers will review and
respond to reports and may take any action they deem appropriate, including
warnings or bans.
